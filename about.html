Welcome to Sandy's Basset Hounds

About Sandy's Basset Hound

Welcome to Sandy's Basset Hound! My name is Sandra Schreiber. I am the Owner & Breeder of Sandy's Basset Hounds. I have dedicated years in the Basset Hound Breed. Our dogs are family raised with our children and are highly socialized! All of our dogs are AKC registered along with American and European bloodlines. Our daily lives are dedicated towards the care of our dogs and puppies, and they are very much a part of our family. I truly love what I do with a passion therefore I will not sell a puppy to you unless I believe you are capable of being a responsible owner. Our Bassets are responsibly bred to maintain the form and function of the breed. We follow the Basset Hound Club of America's Health Policy, testing for glaucoma (POAG), thrombopathia, Lefora and MSP1. We strive for puppies that have a loving and friendly temperament and that will make the highest quality healthy companions.

We bought our first basset hound puppy since 1998, to add to our animal family. We located a breeder online and they helped us to understand more about basset hound, we were so happy to welcome our puppy Gina furthermore the breeder issued us a contract agreement and Gina was delivered to us along with her belongings, such as food, blanket, cuddly toy, papers etc... We spent alot of time with Gina, she became a huge part of our family. We were amazed how intelligent, and friendly she was. She played and explored around the house, and life went on with her, we then decided to get another puppy, as company for her. After that we were all so enamored by these basset hound that we never looked back.

Our Objectives

Our goal is to produce healthy basset hound puppies for sale with well rounded out going personalities. Our focus is improving the overall quality of our basset hounds. We proudly follow the AKC Breed Standards and our parent dogs are health tested. We invest a great amount of time and research into developing a healthy and stable companion for your whole family. Our Spacious home allow us to train and socialize the puppies every day. Our family raises our puppies in our home around our children giving them the best start in life with great temperaments. Our certifications demonstrate our cleanliness and the integrity of our operations. Remember to always work with a certified breeder.

All our basset hound puppies for sale are raised with quality care from day one to insure a happy, confident, well balanced temperament. They are around children, cats and bigger dogs to make sure they will acclimate well with any kind of household. As a outstanding basset hound breeder, we offer a genetic health guarantee, life time breeder support, detailed medical records, registration papers.

Why should I buy my puppy from you?

We are dedicated to raising the best puppies . This ensures that you, our valued customer, can welcome your puppy into your home as your new family member with a clear mind and joyful heart. We feed our adults the best food(No By-Products or Fillers etc), train them, and make sure they are healthy and well-adjusted before we even breed them. When the puppies are born, we offer your puppy the best start we know how to give them: keeping them with their litters until they are old enough to leave for their new homes, offering a comprehensive vaccination and de-worming schedule, feeding the best food available, and offering them all-important early puppy socialization. We have dedicated our whole lives to the pet industry. You can be assured that the puppy's for sale you purchase from us is loved, treasured and pre-spoiled before you even get your first snuggle!
